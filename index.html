Features

Everything you need to manage stock

A complete toolkit for tracking inventory across warehouses, suppliers, and customers.

Products

Organize your catalog with SKUs, pricing, and nested categories — as many levels deep as you need.

Locations

Warehouses, suppliers, clients, and transit. Define zones, shelves, and bins within each.

Stock levels

Live quantities across every location, with batch and expiry tracked at the stock level — not the product.

Orders

Purchase, transfer, and fulfilment orders tracked through their full lifecycle from request to delivery.

Teams & permissions

Role-based access so the floor sees the floor and the office sees the whole. Built in from day one.

Audit trail

Complete change history. Know who moved what, from where, and when — right down to the unit.

The flow

From receiving bay to delivery

Every unit tracked through every stage. No spreadsheets, no gaps between systems.

  1. 01

    Receive

    Log incoming shipments against purchase orders. Variances are caught at the dock, not the month-end audit.

  2. 02

    Store

    Assign to zones, shelves, and bins. Batches and expiry travel with the stock wherever it goes.

  3. 03

    Move

    Transfer between locations with full in-transit visibility. Nothing falls off the map between pickup and drop-off.

  4. 04

    Dispatch

    Pick, pack, and ship. The full chain is auditable from receipt to the customer's door.

The difference

Built around how stock actually moves

Generic inventory tools assume products sit still. Real supply chains don't work that way — things move through locations, cross borders, and expire if you blink.

Batch & expiry aware

Track expiry dates and lot numbers at the stock level, not the product level. A crate of produce isn't fungible with last week's.

Warehouse → transit → destination

First-class in-transit locations. Stock isn't lost when it leaves the dock — it's a known location with a known ETA.

Multi-currency & metadata

Currency-aware pricing and custom metadata on products and suppliers. Handle duties, customs codes, and regional catalogues.
